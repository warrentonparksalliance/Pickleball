Pickle Jar

Everyone's in the jar. Shake it up. — A quick guide to getting started.

Getting Started

  1. Open the app on your phone's browser — no download needed.
  2. Enter your name and optionally your skill level (DUPR rating, 2.0–8.0).
  3. Check "Open to 1v1" if you're willing to play singles matches.
  4. Select a location from the Public Session dropdown and tap Join. Everyone at the same location joins the same session automatically.
Tip: Your name and settings are saved on your device. Next time you visit, just pick a location and go.

The Queue

Player Badges

you — That's you in the list

user — A player using the app on their own device

guest — Added by someone else (for players without the app)

partner — Your selected partner preference

  • Add Guest — Add players who don't have the app. You can edit or remove guests you added.
  • Pause (⏸) — Sit out temporarily. You stay in the session but won't be assigned to courts. Anyone can pause anyone.
  • Play (▶) — Rejoin the queue after being paused.
  • Remove (×) — Remove yourself (leaves the session) or your guests. Admins can remove anyone.

Court Assignments

Open Play (default)

The app automatically assigns players to courts — doubles (2v2) first, then singles (1v1) if courts remain and players opted in.

  1. Set "Courts available now" — how many courts to fill this press.
  2. Tap Assign Courts. Players are randomly paired and assigned.
  3. When a game ends, tap End Match, enter scores (or skip), and those players return to the queue.
  4. Tap Assign Courts again to fill open courts with waiting players.
Rematch: When ending a match, check the "Rematch" box before saving/skipping to immediately start the same matchup again.

Match Options

  • Match by skill — Pairs players with similar skill levels.
  • Minimize repeats — Avoids matching players who've already played each other.

Partner Preferences

In the Settings tab, you can add partner preferences:

  • Tap + Add Partner and select a player.
  • When both you and your partner are in the queue, you'll be placed on the same side in doubles.
  • If your partner is on a court or paused, you'll be assigned normally with other players.
  • You can add multiple partners — the app will pair you with whichever one is available.

Private Sessions

For games at home, tournaments, or groups that want their own space:

  1. Sign in with Google on the landing page (required to create).
  2. Tap Create New Private Session.
  3. Enter a session name and a 4-character passcode (or generate a random one).
  4. Choose Open Play or Round Robin.
  5. Share the link with friends — they'll need the passcode to join.
Round Robin mode: Add all players first, then lock the roster. Set the number of courts and generate the full schedule. The app creates every round automatically — just play through them in order.

Match History

The History tab shows all completed matches with scores and match duration. Use the search bar to filter by player name.

Sharing & Multi-Device

  • All session data syncs in real-time across everyone's devices.
  • Court assignments, scores, and queue changes appear instantly for all users.
  • If you leave a session, your court assignments stay active for others to manage.
  • Share the URL to invite others — public sessions don't need a code, private ones do.

Admin Features

Admins (session creators for private, super admins for public) can:

  • Reset Everything — Clears all data. All users get kicked back to the join screen.
  • Clear History — Wipes match history and sit-out counts, keeps players and courts.
  • Delete Session — Permanently removes a private session (with double confirmation).
  • Remove any player from the queue.
  • View the Admin History log of all admin actions.
