How Pickle Jar Works

✓ Sign in with Google for full access

Signing in unlocks everything Pickle Jar has to offer:

  • Personal match history — view your record across all past sessions
  • Saved preferences — your name, skill level, and partners are remembered
  • Create private sessions — for league nights or reserved courts
  • Become a session admin — manage courts and players for your private sessions

🌐 Joining a Public Session

Select your location from the drop-down, enter your name and optional skill level, then tap Join. You'll be added to the rotation and courts will be assigned automatically each round.

🔒 Private Sessions

Sign in with Google to create a private session for your group. Share the link or session code with friends — they can join without signing in. Perfect for reserved court nights or league play.

⚙️ Updating Your Info

Once in a session, open the Settings tab to update your display name, skill level, and singles (1v1) preference at any time.

🤝 Adding Partners

Once you're in a session, open the Partners tab to link yourself with another player you'd like to play alongside. Partners are kept together on the same side of the court when courts are assigned.

You can add or remove a partner at any time — the change takes effect the next time courts are assigned.

📋 Match History

The History tab shows all completed matches for the current session — courts, opponents, and scores. Signed-in players can also view their personal match history across all past sessions.
